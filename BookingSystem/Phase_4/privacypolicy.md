

# Privacy Policy – Booking System

_Last updated: 2025_

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Booking System (“we”, “our”, “the service”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data during registration and system usage:

- Name
- Username
- Email address
- Birthdate (used only to verify that you are over 15 years old)
- Login information (hashed passwords)
- Reservation history (resource booked + time)

We do **not** collect sensitive personal data under GDPR Article 9.

---

## 2. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

- To create and manage your user account
- To verify your age for reservation eligibility
- To enable reservation and management of resources
- To allow administrators to manage bookings and users
- To comply with legal obligations (GDPR)

We do **not** process your data for marketing or advertising.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

- **Contractual necessity** – account creation, login, booking functions  
- **Legal obligation** – compliance with GDPR (age verification, security)  
- **Legitimate interest** – system security and fraud prevention  
- **Consent** – where explicitly required  

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is **not shared** with third parties except:

- Service providers necessary for hosting or database operations
- Authorities, only when legally required

We never sell or transfer personal data for commercial purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

- Account data → stored until user deletes account or requests erasure
- Reservation history → anonymized when possible
- Logs → retained for security purposes only

---

## 6. User Rights (GDPR Chapter 3)

You have the following rights:

- **Right to access** – request a copy of your personal data  
- **Right to rectification** – correct inaccurate information  
- **Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)**  
- **Right to restriction of processing**  
- **Right to data portability**  
- **Right to withdraw consent**  
- **Right to lodge a complaint** with your local Data Protection Authority  

Requests can be submitted at any time through the support contact.

---

## 7. Cookies

The website may use essential cookies to maintain login sessions.  
Non-essential cookies are only used with consent (if applicable).

You may disable cookies in your browser, but some functions may not work.

---

## 8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Password hashing (bcrypt/argon2)
- Regular security scanning
- Server-side authorization checks
- Access control for admin functions
- Limited data visibility for non-logged-in users

Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure.  
We notify users of any data breach within 72 hours, as required by GDPR.

---
